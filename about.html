About the story

What is Project Starfall?

Project Starfall is a fictional criminal conspiracy thriller set in London. It follows a teenager named Adam who discovers a hidden organisation operating in the shadows of society.

The Story

As Adam uncovers more about Aerial 45, he realizes the organisation has been manipulating governments, stealing military technology, and controlling global systems for decades.

The Theme

The story explores secrecy, power, surveillance, and how one discovery can change everything.
